About Me

Mona's Makeup & Beauty Salon

Certified Professional Makeup Artist with over 10 years of experience

Hello! We are passionate makeup artists dedicated to enhancing your natural beauty. Our journey in the beauty industry began when we realized the transformative power of makeup to boost confidence and highlight each individual's unique features.

We specialize in bridal makeup, editorial looks, and special occasion makeup. Our approach is to create looks that are timeless, elegant, and tailored specifically to each client's personality and style.

When we're not working with clients, we enjoy teaching makeup workshops and staying up-to-date with the latest beauty trends and techniques from around the world.
